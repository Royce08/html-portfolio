The Best Anime Series According to Royce

My top 3 Anime of all-time.

ATTACK ON TITAN

This anime is a complete masterpiece with a spice up of all the ingredients you would require to make a delicions meal, or in this case, a great tv show.

Naruto/Naruto Shipudden

I like to call this the founder of anime, because in my generation, it is definitely one of the most popular anime out there and for good reasons. interesting characters, action and peak moments to give you that excitement accompanied with goosebumps.

Kingdom

I could think of quite a few to fill this spot, but I decided to go with this one. This anime is the stratgic war kind with a great story development filled with epic moments
